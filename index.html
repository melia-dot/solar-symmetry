Install Solar Symmetry

iPhone & iPad

  1. Open this site in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow) at the bottom
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add"

The app will appear on your home screen with its own icon

Android

  1. Open this site in Chrome
  2. Look for the "Add to Home Screen" banner at the bottom
  3. Or tap the three-dot menu then "Install app"
  4. Confirm installation

Once installed, the app works offline and feels native

Desktop

  1. Visit this site in Chrome or Edge
  2. Look for the install icon in the address bar
  3. Click it and confirm

Desktop app runs in a dedicated window

Benefits

  • Offline Access — Works without internet after first load
  • Fast Loading — Instant startup from home screen
  • Native Feel — Runs like a regular app
  • Data Saved — Locations remembered across sessions

Note: Installation requires HTTPS. Deploy to GitHub Pages or another HTTPS host for full PWA functionality.
